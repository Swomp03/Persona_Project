Persona 5 Royal Magician Confidant

Phantom Thieves | Magician

Availability

*Automatic on specific dates*

Notes

All of the links are Automatic

Activation

Automatic: Event on April 15th

*You can craft basic infiltration tools*

Rank 2

Automatic: Event During Cutscene in the First Palace

Rank 3

Automatic: Event after clearing the Second Palace

*Allows Morgana to perform a follow-up attack if Joker does not down the enemy*

Rank 4

Automatic: Event on June 20th

*Morgana may step in to help with shadow negotiation of it fails the first time*

Rank 5

Automatic: Event after clearing Third Palace

*Chance to get an item when Joker performs a melee attack*

Rank 6

Automatic: Event on July 25th

*You can now craft all infiltration tools*

Rank 7

Automatic: Event on August 29th

*Chance to cure party member's status ailments*

Rank 8

Automatic: Event on September 17th

*Chance to withstand an otherwise fatal attack with 1 HP remaining*

Rank 9

Automatic: Event after clearing Fifth Palace

*Chance to sheild Joker from an otherwise fatal attack*

Rank 10

Automatic: Event on December 23rd

*Special Persona Awakening Effect*
