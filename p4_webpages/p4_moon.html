Persona 4 Golden Moon Social Link

Ai Ebihara | Moon

Availability

Sun Mon Tue Wed Thu Fri Sat Rain Holiday
✓ - - ✓ ✓ ✓ - - ✓

Notes

NPC

You must do the Strength social link first.

Be careful with this social link. There are many times where you can break it and weird instances where you cannot get the romance option.

Activation

This social link becomes available once the Strength link is at or past Rank 4.

You will see a cutscene involving Ai. In the school lobby, she will ask you to ditch school with her. You must have at least LV 3 Courage to do so.

When accepting her request, she'll ask you to skip classes during lunch the next day.
