Notes

NPC

Choose soccer if you want an easier time to obtain all the books.

Choose basketball if you want an easier time maxing the social link.

Otherwise, choose whoever you like :D

Activation

Starting on April 19th, go to the head office and speak to King Moron. This will give you an option to join clubs.

After that, go to the West Exit of the Learning Building (Main Building). It'll give you the option to enter either the Basketball Club (Kou) or the Soccer Club (Daisuke)
