Naoki Konishi | Hanged Man

Availability

Sun Mon Tue Wed Thu Fri Sat Rain Holiday

Notes

NPC

Activation

Starting on June 8th, you'll see a cutscene with Naoki for the first time. After this event, you can find him at the first floor in the Learning Building (Main Building)

Talk to Naoki and back the next day to talk to him again. Repeat 3 times to start the social link.
