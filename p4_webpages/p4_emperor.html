Notes

Team Member

Last day to attend: November 27th (You can still raise the social link by hanging out on Sundays)

Activation

Starting on June 9th, at the school in the Main Building 2F, a female student near the middle staircase will mention Kanji is threatening people.

After speaking to her, go to Practice Building 1F. Speak to Kanji and pick the first choice, "Yes I do" to start the social link.
