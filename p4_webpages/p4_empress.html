Persona 4 Golden Empress Social Link

Margaret | Empress

Availability

Starting on May 19th, finish each of Margaret's requests in the velvet room.

This social link does not take in-game time and requires LV 3 Knowledge.

Notes

All of the links are Automatic

Activation

Automatic: Event on May 19th or after, enter the velvet room with at least LV 3 Knowledge.

Rank 2 (Requires Lv 17)

Make a Ippon-datara (Hermit) with the Skill Sukukaja

Possible Fusions:

 (Temperance) Sylph [Sukukaja] + (Justice) Archangel

 (Temperance) Sylph (Sukukaja) + (Devil) Lilim

 (Temperance) Sylph (Sukukaja) + (Sun) Cu Sith

 (Death) Ghoul [LV10:Sukukaja] + (Fool) Obariyon

Rank 3 (Requires Lv 24)

Make a Matador (Death) with the Skill Mahama

Possible Fusions:

 Double Fusions:

  (Hierophant) Anzu [LV19:Mahama] + (Hanged-man) Berith

 Triple Fusion:

  (Justice) Principality [Lv21: Mahama] + (Emperor) Oberon + (Priestess) Saki Mitama

  (Justice) Power [Mahama] + (Emperor) Oberon + (Hierophant) Anzu

  (Justice) Power [Mahama] + (Moon) Andras + (Hierophant) Omoikane

Rank 4 (Requires Lv 31)

 (Hermit) Ippon-datara [LV21:Rampage] + (Death) Matador [Lv27: Rampage]

 (Chariot) Ares [LV28:Rampage] + (Hierophant) Shiisa

 (Fool) Legion [Lv23: Rampage] + (Chariot) Ares
